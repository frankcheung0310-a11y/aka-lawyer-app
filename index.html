I.

Definition of Terms

AKA
/ eI keI eI /
abbreviation — legal

Also Known As. One of the most universally recognized abbreviations in the legal system. Appears in contracts, court filings, criminal records, trademark registrations, and corporate documents across every jurisdiction. Understood instantly by every attorney, judge, and paralegal on the planet.

“The defendant, John Smith, AKA ‘The Broker,’ is hereby charged...” — Standard legal phrasing, U.S. District Courts
Lawyer
/ lO:-jêr /
noun — professional

A person qualified and authorized to practice law. The single most trusted professional title in the English language. Searched 10+ million times monthly across legal help, consultation, and AI legal assistant platforms. The word that closes every legal transaction.

“The first thing we do, let’s kill all the lawyers.” — Shakespeare, Henry VI — proving lawyers have been indispensable for four centuries.
